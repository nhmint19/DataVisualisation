CO2 Emissions By Energy Types In Developed Countries In 2019

Countries:

Energy types:

This chart illustrates the amount of CO2 emissions generated by various forms of energy in emergent nations in 2019 including:

  • China
  • Japan
  • Germany
  • United States of America

In fact, the impact of energy on environment will be analysed based on three primary sources of energy:

  • Gas
  • Coal
  • Oil

The environmental information is demonstrated as a bar chart, where the combination of each energy type and nation figure is displayed as a single bar chart. Besides, there is also a stacked bar chart that incorporates data from all three energy types in a particular country. As a result, the comparison of CO2 emission over a 20-year period could be highlighted.

Because the aim of the visualisation is to highlight the massive surge in the quantity of CO2 emission generated over time, so the data is displayed in a bar chart rather than a line chart, which has the drawback of mainly focusing on tiny changes and overall trend of the data.

Global Energy Consumption In 2019

Here is the chart demonstrating the amount of primary energy consumption in different parts of the World in the year 2019.

In particular, when hovering on a specific country, whilst the others get blurred, a detailed energy consumption data for a hovered country in a 10-year period from 2010 to 2019 is displayed as an area chart on the right section.

Besides, each of the nations is marked by a single colour and being graded on colour scale. The darker the colour indicates the greater amount of energy consumed. The legend of the chart based on a sequential colour scheme with defined value ranges is located on the left.

In addition, users are able to zoom in and zoom out to adjust the size of the map. As a result, data at various levels of granularity could be easily viewed.

Global Energy Flow in 2019

This graph illustrates how total energy consumption around the world is distributed into various sectors in the community in 2019 including:

  • Industry
  • Residential
  • Commercial and public services
  • Transport
  • Other

The total energy consumption is formed based on various sources of energy, each of them contributes to the energy usage of those sectors in the community.
